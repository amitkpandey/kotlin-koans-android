Kotlin Koans - Privacy Policy

As with many apps, we collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, aggregate information on what screens consumers access or visit, and information volunteered by the user. This information is used to improve the app and never shared with or sold to other organizations for commercial purposes, except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission.

Crash logs

We send anonymized information about app crashes to a third-party server. This anonymized information is only ever used for analyzing and fixing bugs in the app.

Questions

Any questions about this privacy policy should be addressed to the developer of the app, whose contact details can be found in the app's Play Store listing.
